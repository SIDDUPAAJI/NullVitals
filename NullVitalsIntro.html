Intro

The outbreak began in silence. Patients vanished, then returned... changed.
You are PatientZero — the only survivor still clinging to humanity.
Trapped in an abandoned hospital overtaken by parasitic horrors, your mind
flickers between sanity and infection. Every hallway whispers your name.

Controls

  • W / A / S / D — Move through the decaying hospital
  • Enter — Interact with doors, terminals, Dr. Cure, and healing statues
  • E — Pick up syringes, medkits, or hidden artifacts
  • Space — Attack with your weapon (melee or gun)
  • Shift — Sprint when the parasite swarm closes in
  • Esc — Pause & access journal entries of the infected

Mission Info

Navigate the hospital wings, unlocking sealed wards with stolen keycards.
Seek out Dr. Cure, whose experiments may hold the antidote... or damn you further.
Collect fragments of the Flame Serum, a weapon capable of burning the parasite from within.
Beware: statues may heal your body, but not your soul.
Your choices determine whether the outbreak ends... or begins again.
